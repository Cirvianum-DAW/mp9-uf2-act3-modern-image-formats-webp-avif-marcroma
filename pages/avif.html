Final Thoughts

I found that the AVIF format allowed for a higher compression rate, all while keeping a good enough quality compared to WEBP. The WEBP format with similar compression rates to AVIF, had a lower quality over the different types of images.
That's why I would recommend using AVIF for images that need to be compressed to a smaller size, but still need to keep a good quality.
