What format is the most optimal for this type of image and why? Can its size be reduced even more? Would it lose quality?

The most optimal format for these kind of pictures is GIF. Since they usually contain simple shapes, and not a lot of color complexity. The size can be reduced even more by reducing the number of colors, but it would lose quality in doing so.
